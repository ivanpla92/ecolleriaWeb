Third generation of glass makers in Spain.

FABRILGLASS s.l. was the original name of the company. Since its establishment, 107 years ago, it had been a leading production reality for recycled glass exporting into 80 countries worldwide. We were the first European company having its own sales office and show room in Atlanta (USA) with an exclusive sales team and the first European recycled glass company exhibiting in China 14 years ago.

In 2009 due to the high cost of energy and the difficult international market situation we took the decision to stop manufacturing.

In 2015, six years later, under our well-known brand ECOGLASS and after a large investment in cost saving new technologies we resumed production in the same factory location we have had for the past 20 years outside Valencia (Spain).

We managed to concentrate in a single facility 1 oven with a daily capacity of 25 tons, our fully automatic glass production, pressed, blown, centrifuged as well as a smaller production of the original mouth blown glass and our 12.000 and more moulds (some of them dating back to the beginning of last century) out of which 8.000 are active.
