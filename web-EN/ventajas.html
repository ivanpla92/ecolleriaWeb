LIVE FOR TOMORROW, RECYCLE TODAY is our motto.

Social responsibility and environmental consciousness have always been our priorities. More than 100 years of recycled glass production has given us a large experience and great satisfactions.

Why recycled glass?

*Because glass recycling SAVES NATURE. It saves row material and therefore reduces the exploitation of natural resources.

*Because glass recycling MAKES US BREATH. It reduces CO2 and saves energy (11.000 tones of recycled glass collected across Europe in one year brings about CO2 savings equal to remove 4 million cars from the roads and about 30% less energy is required to melt cullet compared to virgin raw material)

*Because glass recycling CREATES SPACE. It creates no additional waste or by-products (no land filling or incineration)

*Because glass recycling CREATES NEW JOBS. It turns waste into reusable resources and by doing so supports the creation of stable, local jobs.

*Because glass recycling TEACHES A NEW LIFESTYLE: SOLIDARITY. Solidarity among people (common interest), among industrial realities (circular economy) and solidarity towards the environment.
